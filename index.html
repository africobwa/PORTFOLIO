About Me

Learn about my journey in both creative design and cybersecurity

Mussa H. Mussa (Africobwa)

I am a passionate graphic designer and cybersecurity analyst with a unique combination of creative and technical skills. With Africobwa as my creative identity, I specialize in creating compelling visual designs while ensuring digital security and integrity.

My design work includes logo creation, posters, branding materials, and digital illustrations. In cybersecurity, I focus on vulnerability assessment, network security, and security awareness training.

I believe that good design should not only be visually appealing but also consider security aspects from the ground up, especially in today's digital landscape.
